The Gates

Navigating through the abyssopelagic refuse washed onto the rocks, you get close enough to the enigmatic structure that you begin to notice a soft, ethereal humming noise flowing through the air, produced by a smooth, minty-green panel attached to a massive, vault-like entryway.

On the panel's screen, a large symbol resembling a human hand flashes in a trance-inducing pattern, calling you forward to interact with it.
