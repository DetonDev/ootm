The instant your skin makes contact with the panel, it retracts into the walls, and the vault door slides open in a cacophony of hissing noises.

Entry Hall

You make your way inside and are greeted to what, on first glance, looks like a sterile metal hallway ripped from a certain sci-fi movie of questionable artistic merit. Nothing here draws your eye, and the painfully bright rod-shaped lights on the ceiling even seem to discourage examining your surroundings too closely.

No option presents itself other than to keep your eyes down and enter the room this hall seems designed to lead you to.
