Outskirts of the Morphean

You perceive an isolated corner of cyberspace from it's petrean digital shores. Otherworldly sand flows into otherworldly fields, as your perception flows to a gargantuan, vaguely ooidal megastructure. It nestles between a multitude of dueling, craggy spires protruding from the isle's ruddy surface, desperately fleeing their tellurian origins.

It's gates beckon.
